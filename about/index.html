About the Llama

Fact 1

The female llama can take out only half inch of its tongue from the mouth and therefore it is not able to lick the baby, instead it nuzzles and hums on the newborns.

Fact 2

Llamas are so peaceful and calm animals that even a kid can raise llamas without any help.

Fact 3

Llamas help keep away animals like coyotes and dogs from the sheep they guard.

Fact 4

Llamas don't bite. They spit when they're agitated, but that's mostly at each other.

Fact 6

Llamas are social animals and prefer to live with other llamas or herd animals.

Fact 5

Llamas are members of the camelid, or camel, family.
